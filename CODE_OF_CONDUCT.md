# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations and applies to all spaces managed by this project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that creates a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Using welcoming and inclusive language** that makes everyone feel valued
- **Accepting constructive criticism** gracefully and using it to improve
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community and the project
- **Showing empathy** toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Conduct that could be considered unprofessional
- Violence or threats of violence

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may be asked to cease their behavior or face temporary/permanent exclusion from the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories
- Issue discussions and pull requests
- Community events and spaces
- Official communications
