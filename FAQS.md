## 💡 Advice & Wisdom

### Q: What's your best advice for aspiring developers?
**A:** 
1. Build projects, don't just follow tutorials
2. Contribute to open source early
3. Focus on fundamentals, not just trends
4. Learn to debug and problem-solve
5. Document your work
6. Connect with the community
7. Never stop learning
8. Help others, it reinforces learning

### Q: How do you handle imposter syndrome?
**A:** It's real and most developers experience it. I remind myself that:

- Making mistakes is part of growth
- The journey matters more than the destination
- Helping others boosts confidence
- Celebrate small wins

- Take time off when needed
